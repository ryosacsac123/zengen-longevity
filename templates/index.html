Scientific Methodology & Evidence

8-item Japanese Diet Index (JDI8)

Source: JPHC Study (Journal of Epidemiology, 2016) - "Association between the Japanese Diet Index and Total Mortality".

Evidence: Based on data from approximately 90,000 men and women aged 40-69 followed for an average of 18.9 years.

The group with high JDI8 scores had a 14% lower risk of all-cause mortality and an 11% lower risk of cardiovascular disease mortality.

Gut Microbiome & Seaweed

Source: Nature (2010), Hehemann et al. - "Transfer of carbohydrate-active enzymes from marine bacteria to Japanese gut microbiota".

Insight: Japanese individuals possess unique gut bacteria with porphyranase enzymes to digest seaweed efficiently, a trait acquired from marine bacteria.
