About the Project

1. Our Mission

ZenGen Longevity AI was founded to bridge the gap between traditional Japanese longevity wisdom and modern genomic science. We translate data-driven insights into actionable, personalized bio-hacking protocols for a global audience.

2. The Science Behind ZenGen

Our analysis is rooted in the Japanese Diet Index (JDI8) research. We specifically focus on the unique biological advantages found in the Japanese population, such as the 'Porphyranase' enzyme discovered in gut microbes (Bacteroides plebeius), which allows for the efficient digestion of marine polysaccharides.

(Reference: Nature, 2010; Hehemann et al., Transfer of carbohydrate-active enzymes from marine bacteria to Japanese gut microbiota)

3. The Team

The project is led by a Graduate Researcher at Hokkaido University, specializing in Environmental Engineering. By combining environmental data analysis with longevity science, we aim to provide the most scientifically rigorous wellness guidance available.

4. Affiliate Disclosure

To support our ongoing research, we participate in affiliate programs (e.g., Amazon Associates). We only recommend high-quality products that align with our scientific protocols.
